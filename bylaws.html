bylaws:v2:preamble Preamble 1 section
bylaws:v2:preamble Preamble SEEDED

RegenHub, LCA is a public benefit limited cooperative association, organized pursuant to the Colorado Uniform Limited Cooperative Association Act (ULCAA), C.R.S. Title 7, Article 58.

The cooperative's business shall be conducted on a cooperative basis for the mutual benefit of its members, in accordance with the public benefit purpose stated in the Articles of Organization.

The cooperative adopts and subscribes to the seven International Cooperative Alliance principles:

1. Voluntary and Open Membership
2. Democratic Member Control
3. Members' Economic Participation
4. Autonomy and Independence
5. Education, Training, and Information
6. Cooperation among Cooperatives
7. Concern for Community

In the event of conflict: mandatory ULCAA provisions prevail, then Articles of Organization, then these Bylaws, then other ULCAA provisions.

bylaws:v2:art-1 Article I — Membership ~12 sections
bylaws:v2:art-1.sec-1 Membership Classes SEEDED

The Cooperative shall have multiple membership classes:

(a) Cooperative Member — patron member with full participation rights. Contributes labor, revenue, and/or services.
(b) Coworking Member — patron member participating primarily through the cooperative's coworking space and community resources.
(c) Community Participant — access to programming and community events. May participate in governance as determined by the Board.
(d) Investor Member — non-voting member contributing capital. No voting rights unless also qualifying as patron member in another class.

bylaws:v2:art-1.sec-2 Members SEEDED

Any natural person or qualifying entity may apply for membership, subject to Board acceptance, upon meeting nine enumerated eligibility requirements:

sub-1 · Age / residency / legal capacity
sub-2 · Ability to supply goods or services, or otherwise patronize
sub-3 · Commitment to ongoing patronage as co-owner
sub-4 · Agreement to purchase one share of Member Stock (Schedule A price)
sub-5 · Agreement to pay Member Dues (Schedule A, subject to Board revision)
sub-6 · Agreement to undertake patronage responsibilities per Bylaws and Membership Agreement
sub-7 · Agreement to participate in governance functions and maintain good standing
sub-8 · Meeting other uniform conditions set by Board
sub-9 · Agreement to abide by Articles, Bylaws, Membership Agreement, and Board policies

bylaws:v2:art-6 Article VI — Capital Contributions seeded
bylaws:v2:art-6 Capital Contributions SEEDED
Member shares: Class One (Cooperative Member) — $100 one-time share + $100/year dues. All capital contributions recorded as resource rows. Member capital accounts are tracked in the CIS; balances allocated at year-end in proportion to patronage contribution. Taxed as partnership (Subchapter K). Tax distributions prevent phantom income.
Schedule A adopted June 24, 2026 — share prices and dues now set. No-phantom-tax distribution policy incorporated.
bylaws:v2:art-7 Article VII — Patronage seeded
bylaws:v2:art-7 Patronage and Allocation SEEDED
Six Primitives (closed at Bylaws level — cannot add a seventh without amendment): HOURS · REVENUE · CASH · OUTPUT · PRESENCE · PROPERTY. Contributions → classified as Primitive → weighted by Program policy → credited to capital account → allocated at year-end → K-1 in March. Default Policy covers emerging work before it resolves into a ratified Program.
See Program Stack Patronage Plan v1 and Member Guide v1 for full archetype weights and policies.

bylaws:v2:art-18.sec-1 Fifteen Roles SEEDED

§18.1 defines eleven cooperative roles (guest, applicant, patron_member, investor_member, suspended_member, former_member, director, officer, committee_member, administrator, nou) and four hub operations roles (hub_applicant, hub_visitor, hub_member, ee_liaison).
